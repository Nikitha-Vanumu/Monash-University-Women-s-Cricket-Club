Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Monash University Women's Cricket Club ("MUWCC", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you interact with our website or register with the club.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you register or contact us:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Any additional details you voluntarily provide in a registration or contact form

We do not collect sensitive information such as payment details, government identifiers, or health records through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for club-related purposes, including:

  • Responding to membership registration enquiries
  • Communicating club news, training schedules, and fixtures
  • Administering club events and activities

We will not use your information for unrelated commercial purposes or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Registration form submissions are processed via Formspree, a third-party form service. By submitting a form on our site you are also subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy. We take reasonable steps to protect any personal information we hold, but no method of internet transmission is completely secure.

4. Disclosure of Information

We will not share your personal information with any third party except:

  • Where required by law or a regulatory authority
  • With service providers who assist us in operating the club (e.g., Cricket Victoria registration systems), under strict confidentiality obligations

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Access & Correction

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, and to ask us to correct it if it is inaccurate. To make a request, please contact us at info@gryphons.org.au.

7. Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:
